Alexia Moliotsias
Coach		 Ms. Moliotsias is very excited to be acting as one of the coaches for the Ctrl-Z team. Ms. Moliotsias is currently the Prep School Learning Strategist at Bayview Glen School and is coaching an FLL team for the third time. Ms. Moliotsias has an Electrical Engineering Degree from the University of Toronto and completed her Bachelor of Education at the Ontario Institute for Studies in Education (OISE). In her spare time, she enjoys reading, live theatre, film festivals and of course playing with LEGO.

Alexander Mark
Coach		 Alex is currently one of the three youth coaches of the FLL robotics team, Ctrl-Z Bayview Glen. Prior to holding this position, he was a member of the Ctrl-Z team for 4 years. During his time on Ctrl-Z, he had the opportunity to compete in 2 FIRST Championships in St. Louis, the International Open in Toronto, and the North American Open Championship in San Diego, California. Being in FLL has taught Alex to better public speak and to teach students more effectively which he hopes to apply to help Ctrl-Z succeed this season. Outside of robotics, he likes to play badminton, Scrabble, bike with his friends and play with LEGO.

Justin Borromeo
Coach		 Justin is one of Ctrl-Z’s youth coaches and is currently a Grade 12 student at Bayview Glen. As a member of Ctrl-Z from 2008-2014, he competed in two World Festivals and two International Opens. After his 2014 retirement from FLL, he became the coach of Gryphonbots, leading them to a 3rd place finish at the Ontario East Provincial Championships. This season, Justin looks forward to coaching Ctrl-Z and helping each team member achieve his or her own goals. Aside from coaching Ctrl-Z, Justin spends his spare time playing sports (like volleyball and tennis), instructing skiing and reading.

Christopher Alexiev

Before this season, he was a Ctrl-Z team member for four extraordinary seasons: Food Factor, Senior Solutions, Nature’s Fury, and World Class.
